The Erasmus+ App

A mobile application for Erasmus+ participants

Business analysis, user flows, use cases, content and product structure

A mobile-first service concept structured around the learner journey, managed by the European University Foundation (EUF) in collaboration with the Erasmus Student Network (ESN) and Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE)

  • Developed detailed use cases and mockups across login, destinations, content, mobility progress, reports and online learning agreement touchpoints.
  • Mapped actors, goals, preconditions, success states, extensions and channels for implementation and testing.
  • Worked on an experience model centred on a timeline feed, filters, map/list browsing and journey-stage guidance.
The CCCM Web App

Cultural and Creative Cities Monitor

Technical offer, requirements modelling, Design, UX testing, KPIs and implementation planning

Development of a Web Application associated to the Cultural and Creative Cities Monitor (CCCM) project, managed by the Joint Research Centre (JRC) of the European Commission

  • Contributed to a tender structure covering methodology, back-end, user roles, testing, support, maintenance, SEO and quality control.
  • Requirements modelling included use cases, UML diagrams, UI prototypes and mockups to translate business needs into implementation artefacts.
  • Work packages addressed scope, UX/UI review, target audiences, KPIs, deployment, maintenance and service reporting.
